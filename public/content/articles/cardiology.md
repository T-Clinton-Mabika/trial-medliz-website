# Cardiovascular Health in 2024

Heart disease remains a leading cause of death globally. New research suggests that early intervention is key.

![Heart Anatomy](https://picsum.photos/seed/heart/800/400)
*Figure 1: Diagram of the human heart showing major chambers and valves.*

## Prevention Strategies

- **Regular exercise**: At least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week.
- **Balanced diet**: Low in saturated fats and high in fiber.
- **Stress management**: Techniques like meditation and deep breathing.
- **Regular checkups**: Monitoring blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
