### Triage Categories:
- **Immediate (Red)**: Life-threatening injuries.
- **Delayed (Yellow)**: Serious but non-life-threatening.
- **Minimal (Green)**: Minor injuries.
- **Expectant (Black)**: Deceased or non-survivable.
